EYE
The eye is one of the main senses for human body to observe the external world, which can perceive light, color, shape and other information. Through the eyes, people can see all kinds of scenes, such as beautiful natural scenery, artworks, and human expressions.
EAR
The ear is responsible for receiving sound signals, enabling people to hear various sounds, such as music, language, and natural sounds. It is not disturbed by the noise of the outside world, and does not produce bad emotions such as craving and hatred.
NOSE
The nose can distinguish various smells and has a keen perception of odors such as fragrant odor. Do not be moved by all kinds of attractive fragrances, do not hate bad smells, and maintain inner equality and peace.
TONGUE
The tongue is able to taste and distinguish between sweet, sour, bitter, spicy, salty and other different tastes. Do not crave delicious food, maintain moderation and moderation in diet, and do not be affected by taste and desire.
BODY
Body mainly refers to the human body sensation, including touch, pain, temperature sensation and so on. Through the sense of the body, people are able to perceive the stimulation of the external environment to the body. Not to indulge in comfortable physical feelings, nor to fear painful feelings, with a peaceful attitude towards the various experiences of the body.
YI
YI refers to people's thinking, consciousness and psychological activities. It is the core of people's spiritual level, including thoughts, emotions, will and other aspects. Take control of your thoughts and emotions, avoid distractions, delusions, and negative emotions, and keep your mind pure and focused.
